# 🧠 Investbrain 

Investbrain is an AI-powered investment portfolio tracker that offers casual investors the ability to make more informed investment decisions.

## Key features

* **Historic charts:** Analyze past performance data to identify trends in your portfolios.

* **Real-time data:** Multiple market data providers offer near real-time market data feeds.

* **Wishlists:** Validate investment strategies using real market data without the risk.

* **Smart search:** Search across your holdings, portfolios, or anything else.

* **One-click backup:** Never stress about losing investment data. Easy recovery too!

* **Collaboration:** Grant friends or family view-only or full access to your portfolios.

* **Data privacy:** Keep your investment data private using our self-hosting option.

* **Mobile responsive:** Your investments are always in your pocket using our mobile-first platform.
